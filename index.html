About Ossability

Veterinary orthopaedic implants, surgical guides and decision support

Do you want to offer better treatment for your patients? We know that learning a new procedure is a challenge. That's why we create surgical guides and decision support to remove the guesswork. We help you perform surgery with confidence.

Register for a workshop, use the surgical guides, and get decision support. Learn how you can perform better cruciate and orthopaedic surgery in your clinic.

Products

Guided TTA

Treating cruciate disease with an osteotomy is a big step. We have removed the learning curve. Guided TTA gives you precise control of the osteotomy position and advancement. Decision support helps you prevent complications with expert advice on every case.

Learn the theory online and attend a workshop to put it in to practice. After the workshop, get decision support and know you are offering the best treatment.

Guided TPLO

Demanding cases need the gold standard treatment for cruciate disease. We have reduced the variables. Guided TPLO gives you precise control of the osteotomy position and rotational correction. Decision support helps you prevent complications with expert advice on every case.

Learn the theory online and attend a workshop to put it in to practice. After the workshop, get decision support and know you are offering the best treatment.

Support

Decision Support

Complications can happen to anyone. When they occur they are expensive and jeopardize the long-term success of the treatment. Decision Support can help you prevent complications by identifying and mitigating risk factors before they become a problem.

Get Decision Support for patient selection, surgical planning, and follow-up and know that you are offering the best treatment.

Decision Support is available for TTA, TPLO and General Orthopaedic surgery.
